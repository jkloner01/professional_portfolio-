My Story

As a former athlete at The Birch Wathen Lenox School in New York City and then at Tufts University, I have always been intrigued by the intersection of sports and data. My experiences in sports made me realize that data is everywhere, sparking my curiosity in discovering trends through data analytics. This passion led me to pursue a B.S. in Data Science at Tufts University, where I gained insights into the use of data and analytics in business through coursework and multiple internships.



Through these experiences, I became more curious about how computers and technology are driven by data. As a result, I decided to pursue an M.S. in Computer Science, also at Tufts, to learn how to analyze and compute the necessary data to solve challenging problems in a creative way. Upon graduating in May 2026, I am seeking to apply my skills in a collaborative, team oriented, and problem solving role that utilizes data.
